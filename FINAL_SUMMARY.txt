│ 5. 配置示例 ✅                                                        │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

temporal_sliding_window:
  window_size: 0.4      # 40%数据
  rounds: 4             # 4个窗口
  # → stride=0.2, overlap=20%
  # → 窗口: [0-40%], [20-60%], [40-80%], [60-100%]

eval_args:
  split: {'TS': [0.8, 0.1, 0.1]}
  group_by: user
  order: TO

seeds: [2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, 2026]
models: [BPR, LightGCN, SASRec, MECoDGNN]
datasets: [amazon]
nproc_per_node: 2

计算成本: 4×1×4×5 = 80次训练
